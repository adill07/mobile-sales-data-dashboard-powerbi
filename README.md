# 📱 Mobile Sales Insights Dashboard  

## 1. Project Title / Headline  
**Mobile Sales Insights Dashboard – An Interactive Power BI Report for Smartphone Market Trends**  
A dynamic and visually engaging dashboard designed to analyze mobile phone sales, customer ratings, and payment trends across different cities in India.  

---

## 2. Short Description / Purpose  
This dashboard provides a **comprehensive overview of mobile sales performance** across multiple brands, cities, and payment methods.  
It is built to help business stakeholders, sales managers, and analysts track key performance metrics, uncover sales trends, and optimize customer engagement strategies.  

---

## 3. Tech Stack  
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:  

-  **Power BI Desktop** – Core data visualization and dashboard building platform.  
-  **Power Query** – For data cleaning, shaping, and preprocessing.  
-  **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Used to create calculated measures such as Avg Qty, Avg Price, and Customer Rating.  
-  **Data Modeling** – Relationships built between transactional, sales, and rating datasets for cross-filtered analysis.  
-  **File Format** – `.pbix` for development and `.png` for preview sharing.  

---

## 4. Data Source  
**Source:** Internal mobile sales dataset (simulated).  

- Contains transaction-level data including **brand, model, sales amount, quantity, city, payment method, and customer ratings**.  
- Covers multiple mobile brands such as **Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Vivo, and Xiaomi**.  

---

## 5. Features / Highlights  

### • Business Problem  
Mobile phone retailers face challenges in **understanding sales distribution across cities, identifying top-performing brands/models, and analyzing customer satisfaction trends**.  
Raw transactional data is difficult to interpret without visualization.  

### • Goal of the Dashboard  
To deliver a **single interactive platform** that:  
- Tracks total sales, average sales price, and transaction counts.  
- Identifies best-selling models and brands.  
- Highlights customer satisfaction trends via ratings.  
- Analyzes payment preferences to optimize transaction strategies.  

### • Walkthrough of Key Visuals  
- **Top KPIs (Header Section):** Displays Total Sales (769M), Total Qty (19K), Avg Qty (5), Total Transactions (4K), Avg Price (40K), Avg Customer Rating (3.69).  
- **Sales by Geography (Map):** Shows sales distribution across major Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bangalore, Kolkata.  
- **Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart):** Visualizes how total quantity sold varies month-to-month.  
- **Top 5 Models (Bar Chart):** Highlights best-selling models such as iPhone SE, OnePlus Nord, Galaxy Note 20.  
- **Customer Ratings (Horizontal Funnel):** Displays distribution of ratings (1–5 stars).  
- **Payment Methods (Donut Chart):** Breaks down transactions by UPI, Cash, Credit Card, Debit Card.  
- **Sales by Day (Line Chart):** Analyzes weekly buying patterns (Sunday being the highest).  
- **Brand-Level Comparison (Table):** Shows transactions, sales value, and avg customer rating for top brands (Apple, Samsung, OnePlus, Vivo, Xiaomi).  

### • Business Impact & Insights  
-  **Sales Optimization:** Apple and OnePlus lead in overall sales, guiding inventory decisions.  
-  **Payment Strategy:** Balanced distribution across UPI, cash, and cards suggests offering multi-channel payment support.  
-  **Regional Targeting:** Cities like Mumbai, Delhi, and Bangalore show higher transaction volumes, highlighting priority markets.  
-  **Customer Experience:** Average customer satisfaction rating is 3.69, indicating room for service/quality improvement.  
-  **Model-Level Focus:** iPhone SE and OnePlus Nord are strong performers, useful for marketing campaigns and promotions.
